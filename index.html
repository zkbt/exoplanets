Exoplanets!

Spring 2019 graduate student seminar at CU Boulder

Basics

This class (ASTR5835) is the graduate seminar for the Department of Astrophysical and Planetary Sciences at CU Boulder. It is led by Zach Berta-Thompson and Sebastian Pineda. Please contact them with any questions you have about the class.

Basics

In this seminar, students will:

  • Read state-of-the-field papers in exoplanet science, with a focus on transiting systems.
  • Follow an inquiry-driven path through the exoplanetary literature.
  • Practice some skills of modern scientific communication, including web page design and collaborative open-source codes.
  • Have fun talking about science, both formally and informally.
